CITADEL Lab

Computational Immunology & Transplant AI Data Engineering Lab

About the Lab

CITADEL Lab is dedicated to advancing precision transplantation through computational immunology, calibrated machine learning models, causal inference, and immune-driven data engineering. Our vision is to build a global framework for individualized transplant decision-making.

Scientific Focus

• Computational Immunology and Immune Reconstruction Modeling
• AI for Transplant Outcomes (AML, ALL, GVHD, Graft Failure)
• Causal Inference for Conditioning & GVHD Prophylaxis
• Calibrated ML (RSF, Isotonic Regression, SHAP Interpretability)
• Data Engineering for Donor Registries and Transplant Pipelines
• Translational Tools for Clinical Decision Support

Flagship Projects

• Pediatric AML/ALL Calibrated Survival Models
• DRI + Machine Learning Fusion Model
• National Donor Registry Data Architecture
• Immune-AI for CMV & GVHD Prediction
• SCALA Framework for Transplant Decision Balance
• Causal Models for MMUD & PTCy Strategies
