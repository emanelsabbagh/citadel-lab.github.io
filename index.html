CITADEL Lab

Computational Immunology & Transplant AI Data Engineering Lab

About the Lab

CITADEL Lab is dedicated to advancing precision transplantation through computational immunology, calibrated machine learning, causal inference, and immune-driven data engineering. Our vision is to build a global architecture for individualized transplant decision-making.

Scientific Focus

  • Computational immunology & immune reconstruction modeling
  • AI for transplant outcomes (AML, ALL, GVHD, graft failure)
  • Causal inference for conditioning & GVHD prophylaxis
  • Model calibration (RSF, isotonic regression, SHAP)
  • Data engineering for donor registries & transplant pipelines
  • Translational clinical decision-support tools

Flagship Projects

  • Pediatric AML/ALL calibrated survival models
  • DRI + machine-learning fusion architecture
  • National donor registry data engineering
  • Immune-AI for CMV & GVHD
  • SCALA framework for transplant decision balance
  • Causal models for MMUD & PTCy optimization
